Public Transport Trips Per Person - Intercity Comparison

This chart compares public transport usage (trips per person per year) across all Australian capital cities from 1976-77 to 2023-24.

Interactive features:

  • Click on city names in the legend to highlight/filter specific cities
  • Hover over the lines to see detailed data for each year
  • Population estimates are approximate and based on linear growth models from 2023 ABS data
